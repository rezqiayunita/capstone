BREAST CANCER PREDICTION

What is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer is a disease in which malignant (cancer) cells form in the tissues of the breast. Different types of breast cancer exists. The 2 main groups are non-invasive breast cancers and invasive breast cancers.

Facts About Breast Cancer

There's a good chance of recovery if it's detected at an early stage.

Causes of Breast Cancer

Detect Potential of Breast Cancer

Your healthcare provider will perform a breast examination and ask about your series of family, medical history and any existing symptoms. Your healthcare provider will also recommend tests to check for breast abnormalities. These tests may include BREAST ULTRASOUND, DIAGNOSTIC MAMMOGRAM, BREAST MAGNETIC RESONANCE IMAGE (MRI), BIOPSY.
